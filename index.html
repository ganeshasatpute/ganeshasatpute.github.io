About me

I am Ganesh Ashok Satpute, a PhD student in Mathematical Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi. My PhD thesis advisor is Prof. Syed Abbas. My research is in the field of Inverse problems and dynamic equations on time scales.

I completed my master's degree from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Mohali. My thesis advisor was Dr Lingaraj Sahu. I obtained my bachelor's degree from Sir Parshurambhau College (S. P. College), Pune, affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University.
